Engineering Day 2025

On 4th March 2025, we joined the global community in celebrating World Engineering Day under the theme “Shaping our sustainable future through engineering” The event showcased student-led walk around Chuka town to spread awareness about enginering to the community and a public lecture presented by professor Marcel Ohanga. It was a day filled with inspiration, collaboration, and a renewed commitment to using engineering for the betterment of society.

Trip to Weru tea factory Y1S2- 24th february, 2025

On this day, the second-year Electrical and Electronics Engineering students embarked on an educational trip to Weru Tea Factory as part of the Engineering Workshop I (Mechanical Workshop) unit. The visit offered a practical glimpse into the mechanical processes behind tea production, including machinery operation, maintenance systems, and factory automation.
